Games Development:
Year 4

Fourth year game development moved on to much more advanced concepts. The first semester was focused on graphics pipelines and OpenGL, with the focus moving to animation systems in Semester 2.

My Semester 1 project involved creating an interactive OpenGL demo, loosely based on the solar system. The demo was developed from the ground up and completed in December 2014. I utilised Git source control for this project - the repository can be accessed at https://bitbucket.org/danielrogers/game_tech/overview

  
  My Semester 2 project is an animation system created in Unity. It will be a simple interactive demo displaying some key animation concepts, such as rigging, retargeting, meta channels and blend trees. It will also have a small AI portion, for demonstration purposes. It is currently in development and will be complete in April 2015.
